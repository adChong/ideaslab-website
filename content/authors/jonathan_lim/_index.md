Jonathan Lim is a PhD student in the [Department of the Built Environment](https://cde.nus.edu.sg/dbe/) at the [National University of Singapore (NUS)](http://www.nus.edu.sg). His research interest includes occupant centric controls and applied machine learning, currently working on control strategies for bedroom thermal comfort. Before joining NUS, Jonathan held roles at the Singapore-ETH Centre as a robotics engineer and Dyson, as a research and development engineer.
